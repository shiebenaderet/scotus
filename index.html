Our Guiding Question

When, if ever, should the government be allowed to limit a person's constitutional rights in order to protect the community?

Or, in simpler terms: When should the government be able to take away someone's rights to keep other people safe?

As you explore each case, consider how it relates to this central question about the balance between individual rights and community safety.
